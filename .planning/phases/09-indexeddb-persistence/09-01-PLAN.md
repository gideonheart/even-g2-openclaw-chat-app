- Truncate at 50 chars, find last space after char 20, truncate there + '...'
- If no space found after char 20, truncate at 50 + '...'

All IndexedDB operations wrapped in Promises (same pattern as db.ts). Use readwrite transactions for mutations, readonly for queries.
